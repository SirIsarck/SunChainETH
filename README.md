# SunChain

Decentralized Solar Energy Network on Ethereum

Empowering Africa with Clean Energy through DePIN + DeFi


## Problem Statement

Millions of Africans still lack access to reliable, affordable electricity.
National grids are outdated, centralized, and often unavailable in rural areas.
Communities depend on diesel generators ‚Äî costly, polluting, and unsustainable.


## Our Solution ‚Äî SunChain

SunChain is a Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Network (DePIN) that connects community-based solar energy hubs through Ethereum smart contracts.

It brings transparency, traceability, and financial inclusion into the renewable energy ecosystem ‚Äî enabling individuals and investors to co-own and profit from clean energy infrastructure.


## Why Ethereum + DeFi

Security & Transparency: Ethereum‚Äôs mature ecosystem ensures verifiable smart contracts.

DeFi Integration: Allows energy payments, staking, and yield farming using $SUN token.

Liquidity Pools: Investors can provide liquidity to community solar pools and earn yield.

Scalability via Layer-2s: SunChain leverages Arbitrum or Base for low-cost, fast micro-transactions.


## How It Works

1. Community Solar Hubs: Local hubs generate clean energy via solar panels.


2. IoT Integration: Smart meters collect real-time energy data and send it on-chain.


3. Blockchain Settlement: Energy consumption and payments are recorded on Ethereum.


4. DeFi Layer: Users stake or pool tokens for community projects.


5. Revenue Sharing: Rewards are distributed automatically to hub operators, token holders, and investors.


## Impact

Expands renewable energy access in off-grid African regions.

Creates sustainable income for local operators.

Demonstrates how DePIN + DeFi can empower real-world infrastructure.

Bridges energy, finance, and technology into one transparent ecosystem.
